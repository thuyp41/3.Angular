The Wall Calendar

The wall calendar has a tin tag or a spring mount for hanging. The most popular type of calendar chosen by customers is the 7-sheet Calendar, which includes 6 sheets for the 12 months of the year and 1 cover sheet that is the theme of the entire calendar. The images in each set of wall calendars are usually large and beautiful according to the theme. The general layout is that the image will be in the upper part of the calendar, the lower part is the information of 2 consecutive months, the middle part can be a few small images of the customer who wants to print, logo, company name, business, address address, phone number... Commonly selected topics: Country, people, natural scenery, customs, brands, images of business units....
